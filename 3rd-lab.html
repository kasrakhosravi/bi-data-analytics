Third Lab

In this lab we are gonna analyze a dataset which shows the relationship between maternal smoking and the baby's birth weight. A low birth weight is known to be a contributing factor to a number of infant deaths, and babies with a low birth weight (who survives) are more prone to various health problems. Here we try to understand the effect of smoking on birth weight. In particular, we are interestanding to what extent smoking during pregnancy affect the birth weight of babies.
